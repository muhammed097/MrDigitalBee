Our Journey

From Small Beginnings to Big Ambitions

A casual conversation between two friends with complementary skills—web development and graphic design. When my partner earned his first payment from a design project, it sparked an idea: why not combine our talents and offer digital services online?

We took a leap of faith and started posting our services, unsure if anyone would trust a small team like ours. To our surprise, clients began reaching out, projects started coming in, and we earned our first income together. What started as a modest side project has steadily grown into the agency we are today.

Though we're still a boutique agency with big dreams, our vision is clear—to become a recognized name in the digital space while maintaining the personal touch that made clients trust us from day one. Our story is still being written, but every project brings us one step closer to becoming the full-service digital partner we aspire to be.

// Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our services and processes.

What services does MrDigitalBee offer?

+

MrDigitalBee specializes in website development, logo design, and digital marketing services. We create responsive websites, design memorable logos, and help businesses establish a strong online presence through effective digital strategies.

How long does it take to complete a website?

+

Our website development timeline typically ranges from 2-6 weeks, depending on the project's complexity, requirements, and the client's feedback turnaround time. Simple landing pages may be quicker, while e-commerce or custom functionality websites take longer.

What is your pricing structure?

+

We offer custom quotes based on your specific project requirements. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. We provide detailed proposals outlining all costs before beginning any work. Contact us for a free consultation to discuss your project needs and budget.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

+

Yes, we offer website maintenance packages to keep your site secure, updated, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. We can tailor a maintenance plan to suit your specific needs.

How many revisions do you offer for logo design?

+

Our standard logo design package includes up to 3 initial concepts and 3 rounds of revisions. We work closely with you to understand your brand vision and ensure the final design meets your expectations. Additional revision rounds can be arranged if needed.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

+

Absolutely! All websites we develop are fully responsive and mobile-friendly by default. We ensure your site looks great and functions perfectly on all devices, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones.
